About Us


BRILAS HIGH SCHOOL,IBADAN is a co-educational day school for Children aged 9years and above.
At Brilas High School,we offer a broad and balanced education within a friendly, caring and happy environment.
We also ensure the child grows in other aspects and develops their inborn talents by engaging in meaningful extracurricular activities.
We focus on bringing out the hidden talents of the child and also equip the child with what he needs in order to become a better version of himself in the long run.
At Brilas High School,the curriculum offered is based on the English national curriculum with good referencre made to indigenous learning experience.In addition,our approach to education creates a strong base for future development.This encourages a culture of Independent learning that challenges students to accept greater responsibility for their learning.
At Brilas High School, our highly personalised learner system provides a distinct process, through which student's strenghts, potentials ,ability and interest are identified and harnessed.
